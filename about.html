Who we are

TW Studio is a newly founded Scandinavian brand focused on creating stylish, functional, and minimal training apparel.

The company is led by Tilda Wahlén, an elite track and field athlete and aspiring engineer studying at Chalmers University of Technology.

TW Studio blends athletic performance with elegant design, aiming to inspire movement and confidence through thoughtfully crafted clothing.
